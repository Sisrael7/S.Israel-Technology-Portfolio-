# PHY 150 — Introductory Physics

Hands-on labs and a design project applying mechanics and energy conservation.

- `RollerCoaster_Energy_Momentum_Project.docx` — kinetic/potential energy and
  momentum across a multi-hill coaster, plus an end-of-ride collision model
- `Newtons_Second_Law_Lab.docx` — experimental data, acceleration from distance/time, F = ma

**Skills:** energy conservation, momentum, experimental method, scientific writing
